YouTube & Digital Distribution

The Lasso Group

The Lasso Group is a specialist digital distribution company with expertise in YouTube Channel Management, Digital Rights Management, Digital Anti-Piracy, and multi-platform digital distribution. Their client roster includes the production team behind Baby Shark, the most-watched video in YouTube’s history with over 15 billion views, giving Wiggle Wood direct access to the most relevant experience in children’s digital distribution available anywhere in the market. Lasso will manage the Wiggle Wood YouTube channel end-to-end, overseeing channel strategy, content scheduling, audience development, and digital rights. In addition, Lasso will distribute Wiggle Wood content through Little Amigo, their own established children’s animation channel, with over 700,000 subscribers, providing immediate audience reach and accelerated early discovery from the moment of launch.

International Distribution & Sales

Canoe Film - Caroline Stern

Wiggle Wood’s international distribution and sales strategy is led by Caroline Stern, Founder of Canoe Film, a specialist in international film and television distribution with over 20 years of senior executive experience. Beginning her career at a boutique sales agency in Vancouver in 2001, she progressed to head sales and acquisitions before relocating to London, where she ran the international sales division at Kaleidoscope for seven years. In 2017 she founded Canoe Film. Across her career she has handled global distribution on over 250 titles. Key credits include Sky TV’s 3D family documentary The Penguin King, narrated by David Attenborough; the BBC adaptation of Michael Morpurgo’s On Angel Wings, illustrated by Quentin Blake; and the emerging IP Master Moley, now airing on Boomerang and ZDF internationally. She leads international territory strategy, broadcaster and platform conversations, and the phased rollout plan.

For over three centuries, Coutts has stood beside some of the world’s most influential individuals, families, and institutions. Production Insurance & Completion Bond

Paterson James - Bob Thompson

Securing production insurance for animation requires specialist expertise that the majority of insurers simply do not possess. Paterson James is a recognised specialist in animation completion bonding, a 100% independently owned UK company, operating without risk of conflict of interest. A completion bond is a financial guarantee that a production will be filmed, completed, and delivered by an agreed date to a high technical standard. If a production exceeds its budget, the bulk of any over-costs become the Completion Guarantor’s responsibility rather than the producer’s or investor’s. For investors in Wiggle Wood, its involvement represents a fundamental layer of financial protection. Wiggle Wood’s relationship with Paterson James includes direct access to Bob Thompson, their animation specialist consultant. Bob brings 30 years of experience in production and distribution, began his career at the BBC, and joined LEGO as Head of Film and Story, where he co-created the BIONICLE franchise. Working directly on behalf of the underwriter, Bob acts as a senior, experienced backstop throughout production.

Collections Agency

Fintage House

Fintage House is a privately owned global film and television rights company, operating across collection account management, secondary rights licensing, and royalty administration for film and TV content worldwide. For Wiggle Wood, Fintage House will provide Collection Account Management (CAM): a service that ensures every party with a financial interest in the production, from the producer to investors to profit participants, receives their pre-agreed share of revenues in a transparent, independently managed process. All income flows through a single collection account administered by Fintage House, with distributions made directly to each party in line with agreed terms. For investors, this structure provides a meaningful layer of financial transparency and governance.
