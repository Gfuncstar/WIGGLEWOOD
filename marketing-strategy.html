Marketing Strategy

Wiggle Wood has been designed from the outset to scale rapidly and reach a global family audience. Marketing is a core strategic growth driver - not an afterthought.

Our Approach

Wiggle Wood has been designed from the outset to scale rapidly and reach a global family audience. Marketing is therefore a core strategic growth driver, supported by a dedicated budget and long-term brand-building approach to accelerate awareness, drive audience acquisition, and establish Wiggle Wood as a leading international preschool franchise quickly.

Digital Advertising and Organic Growth

In the early stages, the marketing strategy will focus on driving external traffic - parents and caregivers - to YouTube through a combination of digital adverts, talent amplification, social media promotion, and selective collaborations with established creators and children’s brands. Key creative talent attached to Wiggle Wood brings significant existing reach across major platforms. Actress Corinna Brown, who voices Chadwick, brings an engaged audience of more than 500,000 followers on Instagram.

Additional reach will be supported through cross-promotional opportunities with Little Amigo (over 700,000 subscribers), the flagship YouTube children’s channel operated by the Lasso Group. This collaboration will help introduce Wiggle Wood to an established preschool audience while reinforcing a consistent pipeline of platform-optimised content.

Paid Social Strategy

Wiggle Wood’s paid social strategy is managed by ViVV, a specialist digital marketing agency with deep experience scaling children’s and family brands across social platforms. ViVV have developed a three-year tiered investment plan covering TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube, structured to flex with organic performance so that paid spend amplifies what is already working rather than compensating for what is not. Total three-year paid social investment is planned at between £787,500 and £1,390,500 depending on organic performance, with a fixed agency retainer rather than a percentage-of-spend model, ensuring ViVV’s incentives are always aligned with Wiggle Wood’s best interests.

Influencer Engagement

Alongside macro talent promotion, Wiggle Wood will also activate large-scale micro-influencer engagement through Influx, an AI-powered creator platform with access to more than 53 million content creators worldwide. This allows the marketing team to identify and collaborate with creators whose audiences closely align with Wiggle Wood’s core communities, including parents, caregivers, early years educators, and values-led families.

A key element of Wiggle Wood’s influencer strategy is the commissioning of creator-owned content for use as paid advertising. Rather than producing traditional brand adverts, Wiggle Wood pays influencers to create content in their own voice, talking authentically about the series to their own audiences. Wiggle Wood then acquires the rights to that content and uses it as paid advertising across social platforms, amplifying it far beyond the influencer’s own organic reach.

PR Partner: Valerie Taylor PR

With over 30 years of specialist experience promoting children’s content, production companies, IP owners, broadcasters and brands, she has built unparalleled relationships across every level of the industry.
