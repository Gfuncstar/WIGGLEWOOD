Organisational Framework

Wiggle Wood is structured and operated within the Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS), a proven business framework used by thousands of scaling companies worldwide. EOS defines a clear distinction between the Visionary and the Integrator: the Visionary sets direction, protects the brand and drives long-term strategy; the Integrator runs the business day-to-day, holding operational accountability and managing the leadership team.

Within this model, the Chief Executive Officer operates as Visionary, focused on creative direction, investor relationships, strategic partnerships, and long-term brand value. The Chief Operating Officer operates as Integrator, with a single direct line of accountability from the CEO and full operational responsibility for the business beneath it.

The business is organised into three divisions: Content, Product, and Brand. Each division is led by a dedicated C-suite officer, the Chief Content Officer, Chief Product Officer, and Chief Brand Officer respectively, all reporting into the Chief Operating Officer. This structure ensures that creative, commercial, and brand strategy are each led by world-class specialists, while remaining operationally coherent under unified leadership. It is a model designed for scale: clear, accountable, and built to move quickly. Wiggle Wood works with a professional UK-based EOS facilitator to implement and maintain the framework across the organisation.

Strategic Leadership Hires (Planned Post-Investment)

The senior leadership team is being assembled with deliberate care. These are significant appointments — individuals with franchise-level experience in global children’s entertainment — and Wiggle Wood is committed to hiring the right people rather than simply filling seats quickly. Conversations are progressing at the appropriate level, and appointments will be confirmed as investment completes and capital is deployed.

This sequencing is intentional and reflects sound business discipline. Bringing C-suite leaders on board before funding is in place would be premature for candidates of this calibre, who carry the notice periods and seniority that the roles demand. The role profiles below reflect the depth of thinking that has gone into each appointment, and the specificity of what is being sought. Wiggle Wood is not searching for generalists. It is identifying individuals who have built and scaled global franchises, and who bring both the expertise and the entrepreneurial spirit to thrive in a high-growth, independent environment. Recruitment will begin formally approximately 18 months ahead of launch, with early engagement already underway to ensure the right candidates are identified and relationships are established well in advance of formal offers being made.

Chief Operating Officer

The Chief Operating Officer will establish and lead the broader operational infrastructure required to scale Wiggle Wood as a global business. Reporting directly to the CEO and operating as the company’s Integrator within the EOS framework, this role carries responsibility for financial oversight, organisational design, legal and governance frameworks, and cross-functional operational efficiency as the company grows from a production-focused studio into a multi-platform franchise operation. The Chief Content Officer, Chief Product Officer, and Chief Brand Officer will each report into this role. The ideal candidate will bring significant experience in scaling media, entertainment, or IP-driven businesses.

Chief Content Officer

The Chief Content Officer will bring significant senior experience in developing and scaling global children’s franchises, with a proven track record in shaping story worlds that extend successfully across series, publishing, licensing, and long-form content. Operating at franchise level, the Chief Content Officer will define and safeguard the long-term creative architecture of the Wiggle Wood universe, ensuring the IP evolves as a coherent and enduring story world. Working in close partnership with the Showrunner, the role ensures that the storytelling DNA established within the series can scale consistently, maintaining tonal integrity, character authenticity, and narrative cohesion across future seasons, spin-offs, publishing, and potential feature-length development. This role reports to the Chief Operating Officer.

Chief Product Officer

The Chief Product Officer will lead the translation of the Wiggle Wood IP into a cohesive, high-quality product ecosystem, ensuring every consumer touchpoint reinforces the brand’s emotional values and global ambitions. This role will own the consumer products roadmap, overseeing licensing strategy, category development, and retail partnerships across toys, publishing, apparel, homeware, and beyond. The ideal candidate brings deep experience in children’s IP product development, with a track record of building licensing programmes from content launch through to category leadership. Reports to the Chief Operating Officer.

Chief Brand Officer

The Chief Brand Officer will bring substantial senior experience in building and scaling globally recognised consumer brands, ideally within children’s entertainment, family media, or franchise-led IP environments. Operating at franchise level, the Chief Brand Officer defines and protects the strategic positioning of Wiggle Wood as it expands beyond the screen into a multi-platform brand ecosystem. The role ensures brand identity, tone, and values remain consistent across all audience touchpoints, while unlocking opportunities for meaningful partnerships, product development, and category expansion. Reporting to the Chief Operating Officer, the role oversees brand architecture, licensing strategy, partnership development, and audience growth frameworks.

Now based in Cornwall, Nick brings an exceptional depth of craft, a strong instinct for visual storytelling, and a genuine passion for high-quality children’s animation to the Wiggle Wood production.
