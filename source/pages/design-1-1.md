## Body copy
- Plus 3K is a multi-award-winning independent animation studio founded by Lou Kneath, a character animator and director trained by Pixar and Disney with 18 years of experience across TV, media and publishing. Based in Carlisle, Cumbria, the studio specialises in high-concept, character-driven storytelling built to connect with audiences emotionally and at scale.
- Lou founded Plus 3K on the belief that the best animation is made when talented people care deeply about what they're making. That founder-led commitment runs through everything the studio produces, from the quality of the character design to the integrity of the story being told.
- Plus 3K's most recent series credit is My Friend Misty, a Sky Kids Original produced across two full seasons and executive produced by Fearne Cotton. The show is centred on building children's emotional resilience, a thematic focus that sits in close alignment with Wiggle Wood's own values.
- Their animated short film The Fell We Climb, produced in partnership with Anti Racist Cumbria, won multiple international awards and was selected for the BFI National Archive in 2024. Lou served as a jury member at the British Animation Awards in 2024 and has spoken at the Children's Media Conference in both 2024 and 2025.
- Lou is also deeply committed to bringing emerging talent into the industry. Plus 3K works closely with universities across Cumbria to offer internship programmes and hands-on training, creating genuine pathways for the next generation of animators. It is one of the reasons Wiggle Wood was drawn to the partnership: a shared belief that the people you bring into a project, and how you treat them, matters.
- Wiggle Wood is produced using CelAction2D animation software, adopted on the recommendation of Plus 3K, whose team are recognised specialists in the platform. Their involvement in Wiggle Wood began at the development stage and extends across the full production pipeline.
