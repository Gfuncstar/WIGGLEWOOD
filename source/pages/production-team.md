## Body copy
- As Creator and CEO of Wiggle Wood, Aimée safeguards the long-term integrity, vision, and cultural direction of the IP as it scales into a global franchise. She leads strategic partnerships, investor engagement, and commercial expansion, ensuring that creative excellence and brand values remain aligned with long-term enterprise value. Acting as both steward and driving force behind the company's development, she maintains coherence between storytelling, brand positioning, and growth strategy, ensuring Wiggle Wood evolves as an enduring and trusted family brand.
- Aimée has worked as a senior marketeer for almost 25 years, building and advising some of the world's most iconic brands through storytelling. A dynamic entrepreneur and C-Suite executive with a track record of leading both in-house and agency teams, she specialised in film and TV for the last 18 years, gaining a board seat at the global film and TV PR agency DDA as Managing Director, working with Netflix, Disney, Universal Studios, Sony, and independent filmmakers worldwide.
- In 2016, she founded Triple A - Access All Areas Ltd, a global agency that helped brands create transformative content through pioneering entertainment partnerships in film and television, turning sponsorship budgets into equity investments. Her work spans consumer brands including Red Bull, Swarovski, and Hewlett Packard, and A-list film productions including The Infiltrator (Bryan Cranston), The King's Speech (Colin Firth), Terminator 4 (Christian Bale), and Ender's Game (Harrison Ford).
- Wiggle Wood is structured and operated within the Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS), a proven business framework used by thousands of scaling companies worldwide. EOS defines a clear distinction between the Visionary and the Integrator: the Visionary sets direction, protects the brand, and drives long-term strategy; the Integrator runs the business day-to-day, holding operational accountability and managing the leadership team.
- Within this model, the Chief Executive Officer operates as Visionary, focused on creative direction, investor relationships, strategic partnerships, and long-term brand value. The Chief Operating Officer operates as Integrator, with a single direct line of accountability from the CEO and full operational responsibility for the business beneath it.
- The business is organised into three divisions: Content, Product, and Brand. Each division is led by a dedicated C-suite officer, the Chief Content Officer, Chief Product Officer, and Chief Brand Officer respectively, all reporting into the Chief Operating Officer. This structure ensures that creative, commercial, and brand strategy are each led by world-class specialists, while remaining operationally coherent under unified leadership. It is a model designed for scale: clear, accountable, and built to move quickly.
- Wiggle Wood works with a professional UK-based EOS facilitator to implement and maintain the framework across the organisation.
- The senior leadership team is being assembled with deliberate care. These are significant appointments, individuals with franchise-level experience in global children's entertainment, and Wiggle Wood is committed to hiring the right people rather than simply filling seats quickly. Conversations are progressing at the appropriate level, and appointments will be confirmed as investment completes and capital is deployed.
- This sequencing is intentional and reflects sound business discipline. Bringing C-suite leaders on board before funding is in place would be premature for candidates of this calibre, who carry the notice periods and seniority that the roles demand. The role profiles below reflect the depth of thinking that has gone into each appointment, and the specificity of what is being sought. Wiggle Wood is not searching for generalists. It is identifying individuals who have built and scaled global franchises, and who bring both the expertise and the entrepreneurial spirit to thrive in a high-growth, independent environment.
- Recruitment will begin formally approximately 18 months ahead of launch, with early engagement already underway to ensure the right candidates are identified and relationships are established well in advance of formal offers being made.
- The Chief Operating Officer will establish and lead the broader operational infrastructure required to scale Wiggle Wood as a global business. Reporting directly to the CEO and operating as the company's Integrator within the EOS framework, this role carries responsibility for financial oversight, organisational design, legal and governance frameworks, and cross-functional operational efficiency as the company grows from a production-focused studio into a multi-platform franchise operation. The Chief Content Officer, Chief Product Officer, and Chief Brand Officer will each report into this role, ensuring cohesive operational leadership across all three business divisions.
- The ideal candidate will bring significant experience in scaling media, entertainment, or IP-driven businesses, with a strong command of financial management, operational strategy, and the organisational requirements of a rapidly growing company. Experience working across content production, licensing, or franchise development would be a strong advantage.
- This appointment will be made following investment, with timing aligned to the transition from production into full commercial operation.
- The Chief Content Officer will bring significant senior experience in developing and scaling global children's franchises, with a proven track record in shaping story worlds that extend successfully across series, publishing, licensing, and long-form content. This appointment will require deep expertise in children's storytelling, franchise architecture, and multi-platform content strategy, typically developed through decades of leadership within major studios, broadcasters, or global IP-driven entertainment companies.
- Operating at franchise level, the Chief Content Officer will define and safeguard the long-term creative architecture of the Wiggle Wood universe, ensuring the IP evolves as a coherent and enduring story world capable of expanding across multiple formats, platforms, and generations. Working in close partnership with the Showrunner, the role ensures that the storytelling DNA established within the series can scale consistently, maintaining tonal integrity, character authenticity, and narrative cohesion across future seasons, spin-offs, publishing, and potential feature-length development.
- This role requires both creative authority and strategic perspective, combining editorial judgement with a deep understanding of how narrative ecosystems translate into long-term brand equity. The Chief Content Officer will play a central role in ensuring Wiggle Wood grows with clarity, consistency, and cultural relevance, supporting the ambition to build a globally recognised, multi-platform children's IP with enduring value. This role reports to the Chief Operating Officer.
- The Chief Product Officer will lead the translation of the Wiggle Wood IP into a cohesive, high-quality product ecosystem, ensuring every consumer touchpoint reinforces the brand's emotional values and global ambitions.
- This role will own the consumer products roadmap, overseeing licensing strategy, category development, and retail partnerships across toys, publishing, apparel, homeware, and beyond. Reporting to the Chief Operating Officer, the CPO will work in close collaboration with the Chief Brand Officer to ensure product integrity, and with the wider leadership team to align product timelines with production and distribution milestones.
- The ideal candidate brings deep experience in children's IP product development, with a track record of building licensing programmes from content launch through to category leadership. Familiarity with both the UK and international licensing landscape is essential, alongside strong relationships with retail buyers and licensee partners.
- As Wiggle Wood scales into a global brand, the CPO will be central to building the commercial infrastructure that transforms content success into lasting revenue across categories.
- The Chief Brand Officer will bring substantial senior experience in building and scaling globally recognised consumer brands, ideally within children's entertainment, family media, or franchise-led IP environments. This role requires a proven track record of translating storytelling properties into culturally resonant brands with strong commercial performance across licensing, partnerships, publishing, music, retail, and live experiences.
- Operating at franchise level, the Chief Brand Officer defines and protects the strategic positioning of Wiggle Wood as it expands beyond the screen into a multi-platform brand ecosystem. The role ensures brand identity, tone, and values remain consistent across all audience touchpoints, while unlocking opportunities for meaningful partnerships, product development, and category expansion that strengthen both audience affinity and long-term enterprise value.
- Reporting to the Chief Operating Officer, the Chief Brand Officer ensures creative and commercial strategy remain aligned across the leadership team, enabling Wiggle Wood to grow as a trusted, emotionally resonant family brand with global relevance. The role oversees brand architecture, licensing strategy, partnership development, and audience growth frameworks, ensuring the IP evolves with clarity and cohesion as new revenue streams emerge.
- This appointment requires both strategic judgement and cultural sensitivity, balancing commercial ambition with brand integrity. With responsibility for shaping how Wiggle Wood is experienced beyond the series itself, the Chief Brand Officer plays a central role in building sustained audience connection, supporting scalable monetisation, and establishing Wiggle Wood as a long-term franchise with enduring brand equity.
