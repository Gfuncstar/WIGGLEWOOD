## Headings
- h3: Marketing Strategy

## Body copy
- Wiggle Wood has been designed from the outset to scale rapidly and reach a global family audience. Marketing is therefore a core strategic growth driver, supported by a dedicated budget and long-term brand-building approach to accelerate awareness, drive audience acquisition, and establish Wiggle Wood as a leading international preschool franchise quickly.
- Targets
- Digital advertising plays a critical role in activating early discoverability on YouTube, enabling the series to build sustained viewership momentum, with target benchmarks of 15 million views in Year 1, 100 million views in Year 2, and 300 million views in Year 3. These milestones create the foundation for scalable growth across music, consumer products, publishing, and licensing, ensuring audience development and commercial expansion evolve in parallel.
- While many independent productions and traditional broadcasters invest relatively modestly in marketing, and the independent YouTube content creators behind CoComelon and Blippi had no marketing support at all, Wiggle Wood is built differently. From day one, marketing is treated as a strategic growth engine designed to build a large and engaged global community around the brand and drive sustained audience expansion across digital platforms at serious pace.
- Digital Adverts and Organic Growth
- In the early stages, the marketing strategy will focus on driving external traffic (parents and caregivers) to YouTube through a combination of digital adverts, talent amplification, social media promotion, and selective collaborations with established creators and children's brands. Key creative talent attached to Wiggle Wood brings significant existing reach across major platforms. Musical Director BRIT and Grammy-nominated Jax Jones has 2.5 million followers across YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram, while actress Corinna Brown, who voices Chadwick, brings an engaged audience of more than 500,000 followers on Instagram. Our Creator Aimée Anderson also has personal connections who are influencers with a combined reach of more than 23 million across their platforms. Additionally, the production is exploring a high-profile voice actor for Bellona Butterfly, which would further expand audience visibility at launch and beyond.
- Additional reach will be supported through cross-promotional opportunities with Little Amigo (over 700,000 subscribers), the flagship YouTube children's channel operated by the Lasso Group. This collaboration will help introduce Wiggle Wood to an established preschool audience while reinforcing a consistent pipeline of platform-optimised content designed to work effectively with YouTube's recommendation algorithm.
- Paid Social Strategy
- Wiggle Wood's paid social strategy is managed by ViVV, a specialist digital marketing agency with deep experience scaling children's and family brands across social platforms. ViVV have developed a three-year tiered investment plan covering TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube, structured to flex with organic performance so that paid spend amplifies what is already working rather than compensating for what is not.
- The strategy targets 15 million views in Year 1, scaling to 100 million in Year 2 and 300 million in Year 3 across English-speaking and Spanish-speaking territories, with global expansion built into Year 3. Total three-year paid social investment is planned at between £787,500 and £1,390,500 depending on organic performance, with a fixed agency retainer rather than a percentage-of-spend model, ensuring ViVV's incentives are always aligned with Wiggle Wood's best interests.
- The full ViVV paid social strategy is available in the Documents section.
- Influencer Engagement
- Alongside macro talent promotion, Wiggle Wood will also activate large-scale micro-influencer engagement through Hypefy, an AI-powered creator platform with access to more than 53 million content creators worldwide. This allows the marketing team to identify and collaborate with creators whose audiences closely align with Wiggle Wood's core communities, including parents, caregivers, early years educators, and values-led families.
- A key element of Wiggle Wood's influencer strategy is the commissioning of creator-owned content for use as paid advertising. Rather than producing traditional brand adverts, Wiggle Wood pays influencers to create content in their own voice, talking authentically about the series to their own audiences. Wiggle Wood then acquires the rights to that content and uses it as paid advertising across social platforms, amplifying it far beyond the influencer's own organic reach. This approach is significantly more effective than conventional advertising because audiences are hearing about Wiggle Wood from a trusted voice rather than the brand itself. It is also a genuine win for the influencers, whose content gains substantially wider exposure through paid distribution. The result is advertising that feels human, credible, and earned rather than produced, which is particularly powerful when reaching parents and caregivers making choices about what their children watch.
- The paid amplification of influencer content sits within ViVV's overall paid social strategy, ensuring that creator content is distributed with the same rigour, targeting precision, and performance tracking applied across all of Wiggle Wood's paid social activity.
- Key Markets
- While the content will be accessible globally through multilingual dubbing, marketing investment will initially focus on English-speaking and Spanish-speaking territories, allowing promotional resources to be concentrated where the largest scalable preschool audiences can be reached most efficiently. At the same time, localisation across multiple languages allows Wiggle Wood to grow organically in additional international markets.
- Partnerships
- Marketing will also extend beyond digital promotion through traditional PR, brand partnerships, and charitable collaborations, particularly with organisations aligned with Wiggle Wood's core values of kindness, diversity, inclusion, and environmental stewardship.
- Marketing activity will continue well beyond launch as a powerful growth driving engine. Long-term audience engagement plans include playlist-driven music releases, interactive digital experiences, mindfulness and wellbeing content for children, and the development of gaming experiences through Territory Studio, Wiggle Wood's specialist gaming partner. These initiatives allow children to interact with the world of Wiggle Wood beyond passive viewing, strengthening brand affinity and deepening community engagement across platforms.
