## Body copy
- The Wiggle Wood Foundation will be an independent grant-giving body established to direct a meaningful share of Wiggle Wood's commercial success toward lasting social and environmental impact. It will be one of the most important expressions of what Wiggle Wood stands for, and of what it is being built to become.
- The Foundation will exist because we believe that a brand asking families to trust it with their children's hearts and minds carries a responsibility that extends beyond the screen. As Wiggle Wood grows, so will the Foundation's capacity to fund change in the areas that matter most.
- The Foundation will be funded by a percentage of the Wiggle Wood Company's own share of net profits, allocated after all production and commercial obligations have been met. This structure is important: the Foundation will not affect the returns of investors, who hold their own separate share of net profits. It will be funded entirely from Wiggle Wood's own portion, reflecting the company's own commitment rather than a charge against investor distributions.
- As the brand grows and the commercial ecosystem expands across content, music, merchandise, and licensing, the Foundation's funding base will grow with it. Commercial success and social impact are designed to scale together.
- The Foundation will focus on five core areas, with a primary commitment to the United Kingdom and selective international reach.
- Families in Crisis
- The Foundation will support families experiencing financial hardship, with a particular focus on those trapped in cycles of hygiene poverty and unmanageable government debt. Many households across the UK cannot afford basic hygiene products, or find themselves in spiralling council tax and statutory debt from which there is no clear exit. These are quiet, often invisible crises: the kind that rarely make headlines but devastate lives. The Foundation will seek out and fund organisations working practically and effectively in this space.
- Mental Health
- Access to mental health support in the UK remains deeply unequal. Too many children and families in crisis are placed on long waiting lists at the moment they need help most. The Foundation will fund organisations providing timely, accessible mental health support to children and families: not support that arrives months later, but support that is there when it is needed. We believe that no child should have to wait to feel better.
- Children and Education
- Rooted in Wiggle Wood's belief that every child deserves the opportunity to grow with confidence, curiosity, and a sense of their own worth, the Foundation will support educational initiatives and organisations that remove barriers to learning and early development for children who need it most.
- Animal and Nature Conservation
- Wiggle Wood is set in a natural world teeming with life, and the Foundation will reflect that love of the natural world through grants to animal welfare, habitat conservation, and environmental initiatives. This includes tree planting, biodiversity projects, and conservation work that protects the ecosystems and creatures that inspire the series itself.
- The Wiggle Wood Foundation will be established to the highest standards of third sector governance. It will be led by an independent board of qualified experts in philanthropy, grant-making, and charity governance, supported by a small, nimble, and highly capable team.
- We are committed to doing this differently. Foundation staff will be properly remunerated, well supported, and well resourced, because effective, rigorous grant-making requires genuine expertise, and underpaying people in the name of charity is a false economy. A lean, expert team operating at the highest professional standard will always outperform a large, under-resourced one.
- The Foundation will be rigorous and selective in every grant it awards. Every charity partner will be assessed on the quality of its governance, the clarity of its intended outcomes, the integrity of its financial management, and the measurable impact of its work. Grants will be awarded for specific, tangible projects with defined deliverables, regular reporting requirements, and clear accountability.
- Impact will be demonstrated transparently. The Foundation will publish its grant-making decisions, its expectations of partners, and the outcomes achieved. We believe that the organisations we fund should be held to the same standards we hold ourselves to, and that donors, partners, and the public deserve to see the results.
- This is not a gesture. It is a properly structured, independently governed philanthropic body built to direct meaningful funding to organisations that are genuinely making a difference, and to hold them accountable for doing so.
- The Foundation is not separate from what Wiggle Wood is. It is part of it. Every episode made, every song released, every piece of merchandise a child loves: all of it will contribute, in time, to the Foundation's ability to do more.
- Wiggle Wood is being built to entertain, to inspire, and to endure. The Foundation ensures that its success has consequences beyond the commercial: consequences that reach the families, children, and natural world that sit at the heart of everything the brand stands for.
