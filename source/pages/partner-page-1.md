## Body copy
- The Lasso Group is a specialist digital distribution company with expertise in YouTube Channel Management, Digital Rights Management, Digital Anti-Piracy, and multi-platform digital distribution. Their client roster includes the production team behind Baby Shark, the most-watched video in YouTube's history with over 15 billion views, giving Wiggle Wood direct access to the most relevant experience in children's digital distribution available anywhere in the market.
- The Lasso Group will manage the Wiggle Wood YouTube channel end-to-end, overseeing channel strategy, content scheduling, audience development, and digital rights. In addition to managing the dedicated Wiggle Wood channel, Lasso will distribute Wiggle Wood content through Little Amigo, their own established children's animation channel. With over 700,000 subscribers, Little Amigo provides immediate audience reach and accelerated early discovery for Wiggle Wood from the moment of launch.
