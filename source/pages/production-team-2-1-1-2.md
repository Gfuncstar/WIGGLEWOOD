## Body copy
- Hypefy is an AI-powered influencer marketing platform that automates the full lifecycle of influencer campaigns, from creator discovery and outreach to content review, payment, and performance tracking. While the platform maintains a carefully vetted internal creator database, its AI technology can identify, reach, and initiate outreach to any of the 53 million content creators active worldwide, giving brands access to the full global creator landscape rather than a closed roster.
- For Wiggle Wood, Hypefy provides the infrastructure to run large-scale micro-influencer campaigns efficiently and at pace, reaching parents, caregivers, early years educators, and values-led families across English-speaking and Spanish-speaking territories. Campaigns can be launched, monitored, and optimised from a single dashboard, with content reviewed before publication to ensure full brand consistency.
- Hypefy's client portfolio includes Philips, Samsung, McDonald's, PepsiCo, Johnson and Johnson, Nivea, Sony, Rimmel, and Schwarzkopf, among others. The platform operates internationally across Europe, North America, South America, Australia, and Africa, making it well-suited to Wiggle Wood's phased international growth strategy.
