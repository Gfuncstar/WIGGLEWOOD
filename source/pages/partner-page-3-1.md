## Body copy
- Securing production insurance for animation requires specialist expertise that the majority of insurers simply do not possess. Paterson James is a recognised specialist in animation completion bonding, bringing a depth of sector knowledge that is unmatched in the insurance market.
- A completion bond is a financial guarantee that a production will be filmed, completed, and delivered by an agreed date to a high technical standard. Critically, if a production exceeds its budget, the bulk of any over-costs become the Completion Guarantor's responsibility rather than the producer's or investor's. Paterson James also provides proactive practical support to producers throughout production, with experienced staff available at all times, ensuring that any issues are identified and addressed before they become problems.
- Paterson James is a 100% independently owned UK company, operating without risk of conflict of interest and providing a bespoke Completion Guarantee service tailored to the specific needs of each production. For investors in Wiggle Wood, its involvement represents a fundamental layer of financial protection, ensuring that their investment results in a completed, deliverable product.
- Wiggle Wood's relationship with Paterson James includes direct access to Bob Thompson, who serves as their animation specialist consultant. Bob brings 30 years of experience in production and distribution, with over half his career spent as a producer, writer, and consultant specialising in animation for film and TV. He has produced and supervised 2D and 3D animation across the UK, USA, Canada, Taiwan, China, South Africa, and Japan.
- Bob began his career at the BBC, working his way up to Commercial Manager for Children's, Animation and Education, before joining LEGO as Head of Film and Story, where he co-created the BIONICLE franchise. He has served as Animation Consultant for Film London and is a regular guest tutor at Pearson University across both the Business School and specialist Animation and VFX courses.
- His role on Wiggle Wood is not that of a standard insurance representative. Working directly on behalf of the underwriter, Bob acts as a senior, experienced backstop throughout production, ensuring that creative and financial delivery remains on track. His involvement brings a level of animation expertise and practical production oversight that benefits the entire team, providing reassurance to investors while adding genuine value to the production process.
