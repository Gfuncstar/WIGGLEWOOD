## Body copy
- Wiggle Wood's international distribution and sales strategy is led by Caroline Stern, Founder of Canoe Film and a specialist in international film and television distribution with over 20 years of senior executive experience. Beginning her career at a boutique sales agency in Vancouver in 2001, she progressed to head sales and acquisitions before relocating to London, where she ran the international sales division at Kaleidoscope for seven years. In 2017 she founded Canoe Film, bringing project development, finance strategy and distribution expertise directly to producers. Across her career she has handled global distribution on over 250 titles.
- Caroline's work in the children's and family space is extensive. Key credits include Sky TV's 3D family documentary The Penguin King, narrated by David Attenborough; the BBC adaptation of Michael Morpurgo's On Angel Wings, illustrated by Quentin Blake, which premiered to exceptional ratings; and the emerging IP Master Moley, now airing on Boomerang and ZDF internationally.
- She brings deep relationships across the global broadcast and streaming landscape, alongside specialist expertise in structuring distribution deals that maximise licence value while protecting the long-term flexibility of the IP. Caroline operates as Wiggle Wood's Distribution and Sales Consultant, leading international territory strategy, broadcaster and platform conversations, and the phased rollout plan that takes Wiggle Wood from its English and Spanish launch into European and global markets.
