## Headings
- h4: Operations

## Body copy
- Wiggle Wood's operational team will be recruited ahead of launch to ensure the business has the governance, commercial, and audience infrastructure in place before the series goes live. These are full-time roles, with recruitment planned to begin at the point of moving into pre-production, ensuring each function is established and embedded well ahead of release.
- Legal and Compliance Manager
- The Legal and Compliance Manager will ensure Wiggle Wood operates within a robust, well-documented governance framework from the outset. The role encompasses company-wide data protection (including GDPR compliance and the management of data policies), oversight of all legal contracts and agreements, and responsibility for the development and maintenance of internal policies across the business. The Legal and Compliance Manager will be the primary point of liaison with Wiggle Wood's legal team at Clintons, ensuring that external legal work is briefed accurately, progressed efficiently, and integrated into day-to-day operations.
- This is not a qualified solicitor role, but it requires genuine depth in governance, data protection, and contract management. The ideal candidate will bring a minimum of eight years of experience in a legal, compliance, or governance function, ideally within a media, entertainment, or intellectual property context. CIPP/E certification or equivalent data protection qualification is desirable. This role reports directly to the Chief Operating Officer.
- Franchise Manager
- The Franchise Manager will support the long-term development of Wiggle Wood as a multi-platform IP, managing relationships across licensing, publishing, product partnerships, and brand collaborations. Working closely with the Chief Brand Officer and Chief Content Officer, the role ensures that all commercial extensions of the brand remain aligned with creative integrity and audience expectations. As the franchise expands across categories and territories, this role provides dedicated oversight of partner relationships, supporting coordinated growth across consumer products, experiential formats, and brand collaborations.
- Social Media and Community Manager
- The Social Media and Community Manager will be responsible for the day-to-day management of Wiggle Wood's social channels, ensuring a consistent and engaging brand voice across TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and Snapchat from the point of launch. The role supports audience growth through regular content publishing, community interaction, and close coordination with external marketing and digital partners to maintain alignment between campaign strategy and audience engagement.
- As a digital-first brand, early investment in social channel consistency is central to Wiggle Wood's discoverability, audience affinity, and repeat engagement. The Social Media and Community Manager ensures Wiggle Wood establishes a trusted and recognisable presence across key parent-facing platforms from the outset, working closely with the Chief Brand Officer and external agencies to maintain brand coherence across all channels.
