- Wiggle Wood benefits from the guidance and support of a group of senior industry figures whose experience spans the highest levels of global children's entertainment, film, and finance.

His deep knowledge of the children's broadcasting landscape and international distribution makes him an invaluable sounding board as Wiggle Wood develops its global strategy.
- Con Gornell is a former Executive Vice President of EMEA Marketing at Warner Bros. Pictures, bringing decades of experience building and scaling major entertainment brands, most notably Harry Potter, across European and international markets. His understanding of how global entertainment properties reach and engage audiences at scale is directly relevant to Wiggle Wood's growth ambitions.

His extensive network across the creative and commercial worlds of film, television, and music has been instrumental in connecting Wiggle Wood with key talent and strategic partners.
