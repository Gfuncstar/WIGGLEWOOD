## Body copy
- Cyd is the imaginative dreamer of the group, full of big ideas, surprising solutions, and the occasional mental detour. She doesn't just think outside the box. She forgets the box ever existed. Emotionally tuned-in and endlessly curious, Cyd is fascinated by everything and everyone, often pausing to explore every possible outcome before taking action.
- Though her tendency to drift can test her friends' patience, her unique perspective and inventive problem-solving often save the day in ways no one else would expect. Her signature move, the Head Spin, helps her see the world from new angles, literally, and when inspiration strikes, she celebrates with a joyful caterpillar Mexican wave and a bum-shaking boogie.
- Cyd loves puzzles, mysteries, big ideas, and Wiggle Pops (popcorn), but dislikes being rushed and the fear of getting it wrong. Her catchphrases, "Slow and steady leaves more time for fun" and "Thinking, thinking… still thinking", sum up her wonderfully quirky, thoughtful nature.
- Free-spirit Cyd dances like no-one is watching.
- Determined, knowledgeable, and never a minute late, Cosmo is the natural leader of the Wigglers, older than the others by a few minutes and endlessly proud of it. Fascinated by plants for their focus and resilience, she is a walking encyclopaedia with a talent for delivering lectures at precisely the wrong moment.
- A straight-talker and a meticulous planner, Cosmo has already mapped the route before anyone else has found their shoes. She is reliable, precise, and fiercely proactive. But her single-mindedness often causes her to race past the emotional and magical moments along the way, and in doing so, miss some of the most important lessons of all.
- She is also, beneath all that precision, quietly devoted to the people she loves. She just shows it by making sure everything runs on time.
- Cosmo loves ecology, buttercup nectar, and getting things done properly. She dislikes inefficiency, waste, and anything that harms the natural world. Her signature move, the Belly Buster, helps her wiggle through tight spaces with comedic flair. Her most used catchphrase, "If you want something done properly, do it yourself," makes her both exasperating and endearing in equal measure.
- Reserved Cosmo dances shyly like everyone is watching.
- Chadwick is the high-energy, high-spirited joker of the group. Born without back legs, Chadwick uses a wheelchair, but he is always first to the finish line. Fast-talking, fast-moving, and fiercely independent, he is driven by instinct, confidence, and a love of making others laugh, even when his jokes do not quite land.
- He charges into problems with no plan and unshakeable belief that he and his friends will figure it out. Chadwick's emotions run at full volume. When things go well, it is the best day ever. When they do not, it is a total disaster, at least in his mind. Passionate, messy, and a bit impulsive, he is the most expressive of the Wigglers, but he always stops in his tracks to connect with others.
- He loves racing, practical jokes, and blueberry juice, but hates feeling ignored or stuck indoors. His signature move, the Caterpillar Curl, sees him roll into a ball and bounce like a pinball, often into trouble, but always with charm. Favourite catchphrases include: "Race you!", "Hope it works!", and "Someone needs a hug."
- Showman Chadwick dances like everyone should be watching.
- Bellona is the warm, wise heart of Wiggle Wood. Calm, compassionate, and endlessly kind, she helps the Wigglers reflect, grow, and find their own way, without ever making them feel led. Like a cool big sister, Bellona is always nearby but never overbearing, giving the little caterpillars space to explore while gently shining a light on the lessons hidden inside every adventure.
- Unflappable and full of grace, Bellona encourages curiosity and self-discovery, often helping the Wigglers find the words for what they have already understood. She believes wholeheartedly in the power of a song, and her numbers tend to echo through the woods long after she has gone.
- Bellona knows everyone in Wiggle Wood. Through her many familiar relationships, we glimpse her own colourful past, reminding us that even the wisest butterflies were once wiggly learners too. Her signature move, the Butterfly FlutterBy, is an elegant airborne pirouette that captures her grace, energy, and joy.
- Favourite phrases include: "What can we learn?", "Tell us your story," and "You teach me."
- Timeless Bellona dances like she has always known the steps.
- Wiggle Wood is alive with eccentric and endearing visitors, each one bringing fresh challenges, humour, and life lessons to the Wigglers.
- Luna Glow-worm
- Luna is the emotional heart of the pilot episode. Shy, sweet, and quietly determined, she is a glow-worm who has convinced herself that her light is not bright enough to be worth sharing. What she discovers, through friendship and a little courage, is that the very glow she has been hiding is the most beautiful thing about her. Luna is the kind of character children immediately want to protect and parents immediately recognise. She has already won over test audiences, and is set to become a recurring presence in the Wiggle Wood world.
- Robin Robin
- Theatrical, opinionated, and ever so slightly pompous, Robin Robin carries himself with the air of a retired stage actor who has never quite retired. He is tender beneath the bluster, and endlessly entertaining because of the gap between the two.
- Oliver Owl
- Robin Robin's nocturnal neighbour. Oliver loves to sing through the night with the pure joy of someone who has never once been told to keep it down. He means no harm. He simply has no idea what time it is.
- Flora the Honeybee
- Always busy, always buzzing, and never quite stopping long enough to think things through. Flora means well. Her relentless busyness just has a habit of creating the very problems she is too busy to notice.
- Nancy Weaver
- A mischievous spider with a genuine gift for spinning stories. Nancy is not exactly dishonest. She just has a very creative relationship with the truth.
