## Body copy
- Fintage House is a privately owned global film and television rights company, operating across collection account management, secondary rights licensing, and royalty administration for film and TV content worldwide.
- For Wiggle Wood, Fintage House will provide Collection Account Management (CAM): a service that ensures every party with a financial interest in the production, from the producer to investors to profit participants, receives their pre-agreed share of revenues in a transparent, independently managed process. All income flows through a single collection account administered by Fintage House, with distributions made directly to each party in line with agreed terms.
- For investors, this structure provides a meaningful layer of financial transparency and governance, ensuring that revenue generated by Wiggle Wood is allocated correctly and without dispute from the point it is received.
