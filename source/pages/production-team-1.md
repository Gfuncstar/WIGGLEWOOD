## Body copy
- Wiggle Wood’s original catalogue is led by Grammy nominated producer, DJ and songwriter Jax Jones, who serves as Musical Director.
- With over four billion global streams, including more than 1.6 billion views on YouTube alone, where he has built a subscriber base of 1.6 million, Jax brings proven digital scale to a platform that sits at the core of Wiggle Wood’s distribution strategy. His multiple UK and international chart hits, alongside collaborations spanning artists from Kylie Minogue to Charli XCX, reflect genuine global reach rather than domestic success alone.
- His BRIT and Grammy recognition, multi-genre production expertise and extensive international network position Wiggle Wood’s music at a mainstream commercial standard from inception. Beyond creative excellence, he brings strategic industry relationships that support future collaborations, cross-promotional opportunities and live experiences.
- Jax is supported by award-winning British composer duo Banks & Wag, one of the most established and commercially successful partnerships in UK children’s television music.
- Best known for their work on flagship series including Go Jetters, ZingZillas, Blue Peter, Newsround and Mighty Little Bheem, Banks & Wag are recognised leaders in crafting melodic, high-quality pop music for young audiences. Their work has delivered multiple iTunes children’s chart number ones and award-winning broadcast credits, establishing them as trusted creators of some of the UK’s most recognisable preschool songs.
- They do not simply compose incidental tracks. They build melodic identity, hook-led, character-driven songs engineered for repeat listening, which is a critical driver of streaming performance and brand recall in the preschool market.
- The pairing of a globally recognised pop producer with a proven, award-winning children’s composition team is both rare and strategically powerful. It ensures Wiggle Wood’s music operates at international commercial quality while remaining developmentally aligned and broadcast-proven. This dual capability underpins our ambition to build a catalogue that travels globally, sustains repeat listening and functions as a meaningful standalone revenue engine.
