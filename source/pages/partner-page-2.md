## Body copy
- For over three centuries, Coutts has stood beside some of the world's most influential individuals, families, and institutions. As the King's bank, Coutts is one of the most respected private banks in the world, combining exceptional financial expertise with a long-standing commitment to purposeful, responsible business. Coutts holds B Corp certification and maintains a philanthropic legacy that reflects values closely aligned with those of Wiggle Wood.
- For Wiggle Wood, Coutts provides banking services and, crucially, advance funding against the UK Animation Tax Credit. The UK Animation Tax Credit allows qualifying animation productions to reclaim 39% of their qualifying production expenditure. Coutts advances this credit against confirmed production spend, providing Wiggle Wood with meaningful capital efficiency that reduces the net cost of production and strengthens the financial model for investors. This structure means a substantial portion of production funding is effectively underpinned by the UK tax system, reducing investor risk and improving the return profile of the investment.
