## Body copy
- Now: Pilot Complete
- The Wiggle Wood pilot episode, Show Your Glow , has been completed. It introduces Luna Glow-worm, a key emotional character within the Wiggle Wood world, and serves as the creative and commercial proof of concept for the series. The pilot demonstrates the tone, animation quality and emotional clarity that define Wiggle Wood, and forms the centrepiece of the investor proposition.
- June – October 2026: Investment Round
- Wiggle Wood goes out to investors in June 2026, with the objective of closing investment by October 2026. This window is deliberately focused: the team, the creative, the commercial strategy and the financial model are all in place, and the pilot provides investors with a tangible demonstration of the brand's quality and potential. Securing investment within this period is the critical path for the entire production and launch schedule.
- October 2026 – April 2027: Pre-Production
- With investment secured, Wiggle Wood moves immediately into a six-month pre-production phase. During this period, a minimum of 26 episode scripts will be drafted, refined and tested with real preschool audiences to ensure the stories land with the children they are made for. Audience testing at script stage is a deliberate investment in quality, reducing creative risk before a single frame of animation is committed to screen.
- Pre-production also covers character development, storyboarding, voice recording scheduling, music composition and the full production infrastructure required to sustain a 52-episode annual output. By the end of this phase, Wiggle Wood will be fully equipped to move into continuous production without interruption.
- April 2027 – April 2028: Production
- Production begins in April 2027, with the goal of completing all 52 episodes of Season One within 12 months. This is an ambitious but deliberate schedule. Wiggle Wood is designed as a production machine: the format, style, creative rules and team are all built to sustain weekly output at consistent quality. Producing a full season in advance of launch is not just operationally important - it is strategically essential. It gives the YouTube channel an unbroken release runway from day one, which is critical to algorithmic growth and audience retention.
- May 2028: YouTube Launch
- The first episode of Wiggle Wood launches on YouTube in May 2028. From that point, one new episode is published every week without pause. Consistency is not a production preference, it is a commercial requirement. The YouTube discovery system rewards channels that publish reliably and regularly, progressively recommending content to larger and more precisely matched audiences over time. Once Wiggle Wood begins its weekly cycle, it does not stop.
- May 2028 - May 2029: Season One
- Fifty-two episodes release weekly from launch, building algorithmic momentum and audience scale on YouTube. Marketing investment in Year One targets 15 million views, with English and Spanish releases reaching families across the UK, US, Ireland, Australia, New Zealand, and Latin America. By the close of Season One, Wiggle Wood has twelve months of real performance data, a growing and loyal audience, and the commercial foundations in place to begin licensing, merchandise, and broadcast conversations from a position of demonstrated strength. Production of Season Two runs in parallel throughout this period, maintaining the unbroken content pipeline.
- May 2029 - May 2030: Season Two
- Season Two expands Wiggle Wood's international footprint into key European territories, with marketing investment scaling alongside audience growth toward a target of 100 million views in Year Two. Broadcast and streaming conversations with global platforms progress, supported by proven viewing figures. Consumer products, publishing, and music licensing begin generating meaningful revenue as the brand enters its first full commercial phase. Production of Season Three runs alongside release.
- May 2030 - May 2031: Season Three
- By Season Three, Wiggle Wood is operating as an established global children's brand. Year Three targets 300 million views across an expanding network of language versions and territories. Active licensing, a growing consumer products ecosystem, and broadcast partnerships across multiple markets reflect the full commercial potential of the IP coming into focus. The three-year investment period concludes with Wiggle Wood positioned for long-term franchise development across content, music, merchandise, gaming, publishing, and live experiences.
