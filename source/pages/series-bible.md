## Body copy
- The Wiggle Wood Series Bible is the creative and strategic backbone of the brand, designed to give writers, animators, producers, and partners a fast, comprehensive understanding of the world, its characters, and its commercial intent. While more concise than the full business plan, it serves as an essential guide for anyone joining the project, aligning all collaborators around a shared vision.
- The bible covers every core aspect of the IP, a brand overview, target audience, and tone of voice, detailed character breakdowns that explore how each Wiggler thinks, feels, and behaves, and an in-depth look at the world of Wiggle Wood , including key locations such as Caterpillar Cubby, Wiggle Wood Way, and Pebble Brook. It also sets out the series format, explaining the consistent episode structure, musical elements, and emotional journey within every story.
- Creators will also find the “Wiggle Wood Rules”, a set of editorial and creative principles that define what can and can’t happen in this world, ensuring content remains safe, meaningful, and aligned with our brand values. Additional sections cover the YouTube-first release strategy, searchable content alignment, and Kid Industries' brand love model, reinforcing how Wiggle Wood is built to engage and scale across digital platforms.
- The bible includes sample storylines, episodic springboards, music strategy, running orders, and a full breakdown of the production team, making it a one-stop, living document for onboarding creatives and communicating the creative and commercial balance of the project to potential investors.
- A downloadable version is available for investors seeking deeper insight into the brand, format, and market positioning of Wiggle Wood.
