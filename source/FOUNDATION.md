# Wiggle Wood — Rebuild Foundation

One-line: a music-led preschool animation IP (ages 3-5) + investor data room. The rebuild is a bright, premium brand showcase that doubles as the pitch.

## Positioning
- "A wholesome animated series promoting kindness, diversity, and inclusion."
- Music-led, modern take on Aesop's Fables. 10-minute episodes. YouTube-first (CoComelon / Blippi model), broadcast-quality.
- Owner: The Wiggle Wood Company (TWWC), wholly owned by Aimée Anderson.
- Vision: by 2035, the #1 children's IP in the world, £1bn annual revenue.

## The World
Wiggle Wood — traditional UK-inspired woodland, changing seasons. Key locations:
- **Caterpillar Cubby** — home/school in the nook of a 700-year-old Great Oak (treehouse art available).
- **Wiggle Wood Way** — the main forest path.
- **Pebble Brook** — a shallow, safe stream for leaf-boat races.

## Episode format (10 min)
1. **The Emotional Trigger** — a Wiggler hits a familiar big feeling.
2. **Wiggle It Out** — signature musical centrepiece; they sing/move and land somewhere new.
3. **The Guest Character** — meet a woodland resident whose problem mirrors their own; learn through empathy.
4. **Return to Caterpillar Cubby** — reflect with Bellona; reflective song.
5. **Mindfulness Moment** — short meditation over credits (self-worth, calm, gratitude).
Developed with **Dr Jacqueline Harding** (early-childhood development; ex-BBC Education Editor).
