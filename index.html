Welcome to the wood

A modern take on Aesop’s Fables, told through song.

Wiggle Wood is a music-led animated series where every adventure is a mirror into a child’s own life. By helping the friends they meet, the characters learn to navigate their emotions, their abilities and their friendships, and each episode shares its discovery through an original, irresistible song.

The world

Welcome to the world of Wiggle Wood.

A rich, traditional woodland home to insects, birds and small mammals, shaped by the changing seasons. Some are lifelong residents, others are simply passing through, and all of them need a little help from the smallest, most ingenious creatures in the woods.

Caterpillar Cubby

Part home, part school, built from caterpillar silk in the nook of the Great Oak. Bellona teaches her Wigglers here, and the roof opens up for stargazing.

Wiggle Wood Way

The main forest path winding past the Great Oak. A gentle highway where visitors arrive on foot or wing, bringing fresh stories to the woods.

Pebble Brook

A slow, shallow stream that meanders through the forest. The Wigglers’ favourite spot to race leaf boats and splash in the sunshine.

Four Seasons

Spring blooms to winter hibernation. The seasonal rhythm opens the door to specials and nature-led themes, grounding magical tales in ecological truth.

The music

Songs children ask to hear again the moment they end.

Original, Disneyesque music sits at the centre of every episode. A curated songbook of ten tracks per season builds the familiarity that preschoolers love, and a catalogue Wiggle Wood owns for life.

What we stand for

A world children belong in, and parents trust.

No imitable content, no overstimulation, no fast-cut tactics. Just calm engagement, positive role models and inclusive values, reviewed against YouTube’s Made for Kids policies and broadcaster standards.

Self-Worth

Every child deserves to feel seen, valued and loved exactly as they are. Each episode closes on a moment of self-belief.

Kindness First

Kindness is the north star of every story. Characters make mistakes, take responsibility and put things right.

Diversity & Inclusion

Chadwick is a wheelchair user, supported by adviser Ally Castle MBE, with authentic regional voices across the cast.

Planet-Positive

Produced to BAFTA’s Albert standard with a carbon report per episode, guided by sustainability adviser Mark Downes.
